**Lunar** is a content expansion mod for Terraria focused on the power and mystery of the Moon.
Harness celestial energy, craft moon-forged weapons and armor, and face enemies that awaken only under the night sky. From early progression to post-Moon Lord, Lunar introduces new materials, bosses, mechanics, and exploration tied to lunar phases and cosmic events.

Features include:
• New ores and crafting materials infused with moonlight
• Weapons and armor themed around lunar magic and space technology
• Unique enemies that appear during specific moon phases
• Challenging bosses connected to celestial phenomena
• New accessories and abilities that manipulate gravity, light, and starlight

The night is no longer just darkness — it’s a new world waiting above you.
